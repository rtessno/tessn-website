# Pilot Interest Intake

## Status

Channel architecture selected; dedicated address not yet configured.

The initial public pilot-interest channel will be a dedicated business email address. The website will not use a public GitHub issue, direct message, embedded form, scheduling tool, analytics-backed lead form, or customer-evidence upload for the first intake release.

## Why email first

A dedicated address provides a simple and auditable starting point without adding client-side collection scripts or a third-party form processor. It also keeps the public website static while the founder establishes business operations and pilot agreements.

The address must be separate from personal accounts where practical, protected with multi-factor authentication, and accessible only to the founder or explicitly authorized pilot personnel.

## Activation gate

Do not add a `mailto:` link until all of the following are true:

1. The dedicated address exists and has been tested for sending and receiving.
2. Multi-factor authentication and account recovery are configured.
3. The privacy notice names the channel and accurately describes handling.
4. A retention practice has been selected and can be followed.
5. The public copy clearly prohibits sensitive technical evidence in the first contact.
6. The address is approved for publication in this public repository.

## Minimum first-contact information

The website may invite a prospective design partner to provide only:

- name
- work email
- organization
- role or team function
- approximate support-team size
- general industry or product category
- high-level description of the escalation workflow problem
- preferred follow-up method

The initial message must not request or encourage:

- customer names or customer identifiers
- ticket exports or ticket identifiers
- logs, traces, packet captures, HAR files, screenshots, or attachments
- credentials, tokens, API keys, certificates, or connection details
- personal, medical, financial, regulated, export-controlled, or classified information
- internal URLs, hostnames, IP addresses, architecture diagrams, or proprietary source
- contract terms, pricing records, or security-assessment materials

## Qualification questions

A follow-up conversation may determine:

1. Does the team handle recurring Tier 2, Tier 3, escalation, supportability, or customer-reliability investigations?
2. Is evidence currently fragmented across tickets, files, logs, terminals, chat, screenshots, or documentation?
3. Can the team identify a bounded workflow and a small participant group?
4. Can the evaluation begin with synthetic, replayed, or otherwise approved data?
5. Is there an internal owner who can approve deployment, data handling, and participation?
6. Can the team compare the pilot workflow against an agreed baseline without predetermining the outcome?

These questions qualify a conversation; they do not constitute acceptance into a pilot or a commercial offer.

## Handling and retention baseline

Until a written pilot agreement replaces this baseline:

- access is limited to the founder and explicitly authorized personnel
- first-contact messages are used only to evaluate and respond to pilot interest
- messages are not sold or used for unrelated marketing
- messages should be deleted when no longer needed for the conversation or related legal and operational records
- sensitive evidence received unexpectedly must not be copied into the website repository or product fixtures
- the sender should be asked to stop transmitting sensitive material and use an approved channel only after written data-handling boundaries exist

A specific numeric retention period should not be published until the founder selects a mailbox process that can reliably enforce it.

## Future form gate

A form or scheduling provider may be considered later only after documenting:

- provider and subprocessors
- information collected
- purpose and lawful handling basis where applicable
- access controls
- retention and deletion behavior
- spam and abuse handling
- cross-border processing where relevant
- accessibility
- privacy-notice changes
- whether the provider adds tracking or analytics

## Website behavior before activation

Until the dedicated address is configured, the pilot page must:

- state that the contact channel is pending
- explain the intended qualification information
- explicitly prohibit sensitive evidence and credentials
- avoid linking visitors to a public GitHub profile as the pilot intake path
- avoid collecting information directly
