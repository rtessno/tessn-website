Draft policy

Privacy notice

This preview website is designed not to collect personal information directly.

Draft for website development. Obtain appropriate review before adding analytics, forms, mailing lists, accounts, payments, public downloads, or customer evidence handling.

Current website behavior

The site is static and does not include an intake form, account system, payment processing, advertising tracker, first-party analytics service, customer-evidence upload, or public software download.

Hosting

The site is intended to be hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information necessary to deliver the site under GitHub's own policies.

Pilot interest channel

The planned initial pilot-interest channel is a dedicated business email address. The address is not yet active or published. Before activation, the mailbox must have limited access, multi-factor authentication, recovery controls, a workable retention practice, and tested sending and receiving.

An initial inquiry may include a name, work email, organization, role, approximate support-team size, general industry or product category, a high-level workflow problem, and preferred follow-up method. This information would be used only to evaluate and respond to pilot interest and related operational or legal records.

A specific numeric retention period will not be promised until a mailbox process exists that can reliably enforce it. Access should be limited to the founder and explicitly authorized personnel, and messages should be deleted when no longer needed for the conversation or related operational and legal records.

Do not submit sensitive information

Do not submit customer evidence, ticket exports, logs, traces, packet captures, HAR files, screenshots, credentials, tokens, API keys, certificates, internal URLs, proprietary source, architecture diagrams, regulated information, contracts, or pricing through an initial inquiry or public GitHub channel.

A separate, approved evidence-transfer and data-handling process must be agreed in writing before any sensitive material is exchanged.

External links

Links to GitHub or other third-party services are governed by those services' policies. Tessn does not control their data practices.

Future collection

Before adding forms, scheduling tools, mailing lists, analytics, downloads, customer portals, or evidence transfer, this notice must identify the provider, information collected, purpose, access, retention, sharing, security controls, and relevant subprocessors.

Contact

A dedicated public privacy contact has not yet been established. The pilot page will publish no address until the activation gate described above is complete.
